# Business Model

## Business Model Canvas

### Value Proposition
A modern community currency platform that combines:
- **Timebanking** (1hr = 1hr, egalitarian service exchange)
- **LETS mutual credit** (flexible goods/materials exchange)
- **Crypto token** (transparent ledger, cross-community portability)
- **AI matching** (skill-to-need matching that no existing platform offers)

### Customer Segments
1. **Community organisations** — housing associations, charities, community centres wanting to run a timebank
2. **NHS/local authorities** — social prescribing teams needing structured community activities
3. **Aging population** — people wanting to "bank time for future care"
4. **Individual members** — community members wanting to exchange skills and build social connections

### Revenue Streams
- [ ] **Freemium SaaS**: Free for small communities (<50 members), paid tiers for larger networks
- [ ] **NHS/LA licensing**: Per-referral or annual licence for social prescribing integration
- [ ] **Fiat bridge fees**: Small margin on cash-to-TIM purchases
- [ ] **Enterprise tier**: White-label for housing associations, care providers, large charities
- [ ] **Training & consultancy**: Helping organisations set up and run timebanks
- [ ] **Data insights** (anonymised): Aggregate social impact data for research/policy

### Cost Structure
- [ ] Platform development and maintenance
- [ ] Hosting (Supabase, Cloudflare, etc.)
- [ ] Blockchain gas fees (minimal on L2/alternative chains)
- [ ] Customer support
- [ ] Marketing and community development

### Key Partners
- Timebanking UK
- NHS social prescribing link workers / NASP
- Age UK
- Platform Cooperativism Consortium
- Local authorities
- Housing associations

### Channels
- App stores (iOS, Android)
- NHS social prescribing referral pathways
- Community organisation networks
- Timebanking UK membership network
